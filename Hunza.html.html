Explore the website about Hunza

Hunza Valley is a breathtaking mountainous region in Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan, known for its stunning landscapes, rich culture, and peaceful environment. Surrounded by snow-capped peaks, including Rakaposhi and Attabad Lake, it attracts tourists with its natural beauty, historical forts like Baltit and Altit, and warm hospitality. The valley is also famous for its high literacy rate and healthy lifestyle.
